Challenge 1

Add 5 buttons. 1 success button, 1 outline danger button, 1 large button, 1 small button, and 1 custom button.

Challenge 2

Add 3 progress bars. Progress bar one should have a value of ten and be red. Progress bar 2 should have a value of 50, be black, and have a height of 50px. Progress bar 3 should have a value of 100, be a color of your choice, and show some text.

Warning !!

Challenge 3

Add 3 cards. Add an image of your favorite TV show to card 1. Card 2 should include a list of your favorite characters from the TV show and the text should be centered. Card 3 can be the same as the first card, but should have a different background color and text color.

BONUS: Align all 3 cards in a single row using Bootstrap's grid system.

Click here for more info on grid

Challenge 4

Add a collapsible accordion that shows a picture of each of your favorite characters from challenge 3.

BONUS: Images should fill the entire accordion, and the accordion should take up 8 grids and be centered.
